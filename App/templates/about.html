About Us

Discover the story behind the ultimate burger experience. From humble beginnings to becoming a global sensation, Burger King has been serving delicious, flame-grilled burgers to food lovers all around the world.

Our Story

Founded in 1954, Burger King started as a small burger joint with one goal: to offer a flame-grilled alternative to traditional fast food. Today, we're known for our iconic Whopper and our commitment to high-quality, flavorful meals.

Our Values

  • Respect for the Environment

Meet Our Team
